About Us

We use primordial indigenous knowledge to ascertain the winner of a football match. Our objectives are to:

  1. Show the power of primordial indigenous knowledge, and
  2. Ignite dialogue on possible applications of this “forgotten” knowledge in our day-to-day activities as individuals and as a nation.

Therefore, this website is strictly educational. We do not endorse, encourage or promote betting or gambling.
